About

I am an IndustiralErgonomist who is a professional who studies, designs, and evaluates human work systems with the goal of optimizing the performance and safety of those systems. And the World's Frist Mobile App. for Ergonomics Dictionary, the Eng.-BM-Eng., has been published by me & my team members in March 2019.

I have ever participate in The Guideline for the Ergonomic Manual Handling , the M'sia's Gov. Project (under Ministry of HR). And I have been in charge of Technical Advisor in the IH2C 2020 Project that boosts up the awareness of ergonomic & hygienic risks in national domestic sectors. The portfolio will show my ergonomic career. I look forward to sharing more information with y'all !!!
